Kakashi

 Kakashi Hatake is a shinobi from Konohagakure. He received a Sharingan when he was younger, earning him the monikers Kakashi the Copy Ninja and Kakashi of the Sharingan. His prodigious talent, skills, and proficiency with the Sharingan made him one of the village's most capable ninjas, recognized throughout the ninja world. He ends up being appointed as the leader of Team Kakashi, where he uses his years of experience to train his students to become skilled ninjas in their own right.

Naruto

 Naruto Uzumaki is a shinobi from Konohagakure, a member of the Uzumaki clan, and the eponymous protagonist of the Naruto franchise. Since his birth, he became the Nine-Tails jinchuuriki, a fate that led him to be condemned and neglected by the entire village during his childhood. After joining Team 7, alongside Sakura Haruno and Sasuke Uchiha, under the leadership of Kakashi Hatake, Naruto worked hard to earn the village's respect and recognition, with the dream of becoming Hokage.

Team Seven

 Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura are organized into a team after their graduation from the Academy in order to balance their talents: Naruto, the worst student in his class, would benefit from Sakura's intelligence and Sasuke's ninjutsu proficiency; Sakura would benefit from her teammates' combat abilities; Sasuke would benefit from learning to work as a team. Kakashi is chosen to train them, to keep an eye on Naruto, the Nine-Tails jinchuuriki, and to help Sasuke cope with his life after the Uchiha Clan Massacre.

Sasuke

 Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan from Konohagakure. He became a shinobi so that he could someday become strong enough to seek revenge against his older brother, Itachi, who had massacred their entire clan. Initially a member of Konoha's Team Kakashi, Sasuke defected from the village to gain power under Orochimaru and later also joined Akatsuki, becoming an international criminal in the process. After the war, Sasuke decides to go back and help the village.

Sakura

 Sakura Haruno is a kunoichi from the Haruno clan in Konohagakure. She is assigned as a member of Team Kakashi but quickly finds herself ill-prepared for the duties of a ninja and the complications of her teammates' lives. Training under the guidance of her mentor, Tsunade, she becomes a stronger kunoichi and an excellent medical ninja, capable of facing the challenges of a ninja's life as well as helping and protecting her friends and loved ones when they need her. After the war, Sakura marries Sasuke.
